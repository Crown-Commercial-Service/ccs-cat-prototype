How you can use this service

This service enables you to:

  • Shortlist suppliers and upload a list of your requirements.
  • Run a pre-market engagement event such as a Request for Information (RfI) or Expression of Interest (EoI).
  • Run a Request for Proposal (RfP) or Direct Award (DA).
  • Respond to clarification questions from suppliers.
  • Manage an evaluation process with colleagues.
  • Score proposals and award a contract.
  • Access and download an audit record of the procurement.
