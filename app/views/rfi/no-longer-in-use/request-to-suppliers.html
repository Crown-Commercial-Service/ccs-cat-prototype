Completing the RFI request to suppliers

{% include "includes/project_header.html" %}

Having set out the detail of the information you are requesting suppliers to provide feedback on you will be taken through a series of questions to enable the Supplier to understand the context for your procurement. This enables the supplier to tailor their response to your specific needs.

Following the completion of these questions you will be able to

  • review the information provided and change it if required
  • review a copy of the document that will be sent to the supplier
  • review a list of all suppliers and de-select any suppliers you don't wish to include as part of this RFI stage
  • add additional team members who can manage the project
  • define a closing date for supplier response
  • publish the RFI details to the suppliers

The information being captured as part of this RFI will be retained with in the project for re-use in subsequent stages of the procurement
